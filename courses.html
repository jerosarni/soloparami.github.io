Courses

Photography Classes with Cell Phone, Styling, Editing, and Social Media Learn from the Best! Welcome to our exclusive photography classes with cell phone, styling, editing, and social media, where photography and creativity come together to take your visual skills to the next level. Allow us to introduce Marina Maiztegui and Rosario Lanusse, two renowned photographers who have joined forces to offer you an unparalleled educational experience.

Since 2017, Marina and Rosario have combined their talents to teach photography classes to over 4,000 students from around the world. They have conducted classes in different cities, from Miami to Salta, and from Córdoba to Ushuaia. They have also taught classes for renowned companies such as Facebook, Yahoo Latinoamérica, Sony, Ohlala Magazine, and Banco Santander.

These classes are a unique opportunity to learn professional photography techniques with your phone, styling, editing, and how to enhance your presence on social media. They are designed to inspire you and help you develop your own visual voice, combining technical skills with a personal and authentic narrative.

To make learning even more accessible, they have recorded their classes so you can enjoy them online from the comfort of your home, no matter where you are in the world. Follow the lessons at your own pace, and discover how to master photography, create unique styles, and polish your images with professional editing techniques.

Whether you are a beginner or advanced photographer, our classes are designed to provide you with an enriching and challenging experience. Be inspired, learn, and amaze the world with your images!
