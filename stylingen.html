Styling

As a creative with experience in interior design and advertising, Marina Maiztegui's work process has led her to work as a stylist for books, magazines, social media, and diverse clients. Marina has carved a niche for herself as a highly sought-after stylist in the creative industry, whether curating interiors for books, creating captivating visual narratives for magazines, or designing eye-catching content for social media, her artistic touch adds a personal flair to each project.

Her background as an interior designer allows her to transform spaces into cozy and inspiring environments, creating visual stories that resonate with her audience. Her deep understanding of communication adds another layer of depth to her work.
